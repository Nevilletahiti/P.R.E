Paper Route Empire abbreviated as (P.R.E), was founded in 2008 by a world famous rapper known as Adolph Robert Thorton.
Better known by his stage name Young Dolph, he was born in July 1985, in South Mempis where he grew up with his mother.
In 2016, he released his debut studio album; King of Memphis, which peaked at number 49 on the Billboard 200 chart.
He was featured on O.T. Genasis's hit single "Cut It", which peaked at number 35 on the Billboard Hot 100.
Young Dolph's album, Rich Slave, was released in 2020 and became his highest-charting project, debuting at number 4 on the Billboard 200.
